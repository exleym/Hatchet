Hatchet: College Football Analysis Toolkit¶

Welcome to the documentation for the Hatchet analysis toolkit!

Hatchet is a suite of tools for managing analyzing college football and sports gambling data. The full toolkit is designed to manage the analysis lifecycle from data ingress pipelines through development of betting strategies.
